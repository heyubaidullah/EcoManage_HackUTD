Platform Capabilities

Everything a building manager needs,
in one place

From real-time energy monitoring to AI-driven cost forecasting — EcoManage turns raw building data into clear, actionable intelligence for property managers.

Real-Time Performance Dashboard
Monitor energy consumption, HVAC usage, temperature, and waste generation across all buildings with live KPI cards and trend charts.
7-Day Predictive Forecast
Linear regression forecasting projects energy and waste levels a week ahead, helping managers plan maintenance, staffing, and procurement.
Smart Recommendations Engine
Context-aware sustainability recommendations adapt to each building's current performance, guiding managers toward targeted efficiency improvements.
EcoScore™ Sustainability Rating
A composite 0–100 score (graded A–F) benchmarks each building across energy, HVAC, temperature, and waste — giving managers an at-a-glance sustainability health check.
Business Impact Analytics
See real monetary impact — daily energy cost in dollars, monthly projections, and estimated savings — alongside carbon equivalent metrics that resonate with stakeholders.
Automated Threshold Alerts
Instant alerts fire when any metric breaches pre-defined thresholds — ensuring issues are caught and resolved before they escalate into expensive problems.
Built for the People Managing Our World's Buildings

One platform. Every building. Total control.

Property managers today are expected to hit sustainability targets, reduce operating costs, and keep tenants happy — all with limited visibility into what's actually happening inside their buildings.

EcoManage solves that by turning building sensor data into a clear, actionable management interface — so every decision is backed by real evidence.

  • Monitor energy, HVAC, and waste in real time across your full portfolio
  • Receive AI-generated recommendations tailored to each building's performance
  • Forecast next week's resource usage and plan proactively
  • Track carbon footprint and align with CBRE ESG commitments
  • See dollar savings — not just kWh — to make the business case internally
